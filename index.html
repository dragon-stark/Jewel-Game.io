Crystal Collector

Instructions

    You will be given a random number at the start of the game. You will have the option to choose from four crystals below.


  • By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number
  • you lose the game if your total score goes above the randomly chosen number number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
